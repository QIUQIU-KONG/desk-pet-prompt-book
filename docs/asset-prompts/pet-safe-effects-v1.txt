Electron-safe desktop-pet effects v1
===================================

Purpose
-------
Add visible magical light and recognizable butterflies to the real 220x220
Electron transparent window without CSS blur, shadow, filter, or blend-mode
compositing.

Glow overlay
------------
Generated deterministically as a 1600x1600 RGBA raster with Pillow and NumPy.
The transparent canvas contains a restrained cyan-white center-spine light and
a blue-violet elliptical levitation light beneath the book. Alpha fades to
zero around every outer edge.

Butterfly source prompt
-----------------------
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: Electron desktop-pet butterfly sprite
Primary request: one elegant luminous fantasy butterfly with a clearly
recognizable, symmetrical pair of fully spread wings
Scene/backdrop: perfectly flat solid #ff00ff chroma-key background for
removal, one uniform color with no gradients, texture, shadows, reflections,
or floor plane
Subject: a single centered butterfly viewed from the front/top, refined white
and icy-blue wings, slender body, subtle cyan inner illumination, crisp
readable silhouette
Style/medium: polished semi-realistic magical illustration suitable for a
premium ancient magic-book desktop pet
Composition/framing: centered square composition, generous padding on all
sides, entire wings and antennae fully visible
Lighting/mood: restrained magical luminosity contained close to the wings;
elegant rather than cute
Constraints: no cast shadow, no contact shadow, no text, no watermark, no
additional objects, no magenta anywhere in the butterfly, subject fully
separated from the background
Avoid: paired glowing blobs, abstract orb shape, heavy bloom, distant blur
halos, cropped wings, multiple butterflies, floral decorations

Transparent preparation
-----------------------
The selected built-in image-generation output was keyed locally with
remove_chroma_key.py using #ff00ff, a soft matte, despill, and a one-pixel edge
contract. The visible subject was cropped, centered, and downsampled to a
512x512 RGBA PNG. The final sprite was checked at approximately 24px on both
light and dark backgrounds.
